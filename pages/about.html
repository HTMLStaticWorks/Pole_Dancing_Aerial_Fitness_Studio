Our Story

From a small studio with one pole to Chennai's premier aerial fitness destination — discover the passion behind every class.

Our Mission

Strength, Grace & Community

Founded in 2016 by Kavya Sundaram, Pole Dancing Studio began as a vision to bring aerial arts to everyday people. What started with two poles in a borrowed space has grown into a vibrant 3,000 sq ft studio, home to over 500 active students.

We believe that aerial fitness is for every body, every age, every background. Our inclusive, empowering approach breaks barriers and builds confidence that extends far beyond the studio walls.
