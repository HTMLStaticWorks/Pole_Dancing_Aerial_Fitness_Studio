Before You Call

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Still not sure? Just drop us a message!

What should I bring to my first class?

Just bring yourself, comfortable workout clothes (shorts recommended for pole), and a bottle of water. We provide all the equipment. Avoid moisturiser on your hands and legs on pole class days — it affects grip.

Is parking available?

Yes! We have free parking available on-site for up to 15 cars. Additional street parking is available on Anna Nagar 2nd Avenue. We're also a 5-minute walk from Anna Nagar Metro Station.

Can I drop in without a booking?

Our classes are small and fill up quickly, so we strongly recommend booking online in advance. However, if you'd like to drop in for an open practice session, please call us first to check availability.

Do you offer private coaching?

Absolutely! Private 1-on-1 and semi-private sessions are available with all our instructors. Prices start at ₹2,500 per 60-minute session. Contact us to arrange your preferred time.

How do I book a party package?

Fill in the contact form above selecting "Party Package Enquiry" or call us directly. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date and package.
