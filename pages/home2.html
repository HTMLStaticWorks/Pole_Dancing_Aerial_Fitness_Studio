FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

Absolutely not! Our beginner classes are designed for complete newcomers. All you need is a positive attitude and comfortable workout clothes.

What should I wear?

Wear form-fitting shorts and a tank top for pole classes (skin contact helps grip). For silks and lyra, leggings work well. No socks or shoes needed on the apparatus.

Is it safe?

Safety is our top priority. We use professional-grade rigging, crash mats, and our instructors are trained in spotter techniques. All equipment is regularly inspected.

Can I try before committing?

Yes! We offer a free trial class for new students. Just book online and come experience the magic for yourself before choosing a membership.

Are there age restrictions?

Adult classes are for 18+. We also offer dedicated youth aerial programs for ages 8–17 in a safe, age-appropriate curriculum.
